Magic Cake

Magic Cake is the tastiest cake on the planet. Few have tried it and survived to tell how delicious it is.

We need to stress out that we don't garantee anything after you have consumed magic cake.

Please take a look at our legal notes for more info.

Ingredients

  • 10 eggs
  • 2 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directories

  1. Mix eggs, sugar and butter in a large bowl
  2. put into the oven
  3. eat and enjoy
